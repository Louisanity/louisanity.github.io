Research

Hardware-aware quantum algorithms for networked and heterogeneous systems.

My research develops scalable methods for quantum computation beyond isolated devices. The central theme is hardware-aware quantum algorithms for networked quantum systems: distributed quantum computing, photonic quantum machine learning, quantum–HPC benchmarking, compiler/runtime co-design, and practical quantum sensing technologies with spin–acoustic interfaces.

The areas below connect algorithmic design with implementation constraints such as connectivity, noise, entanglement generation, qumode/qubit capacity, classical orchestration overhead, and benchmark reproducibility.
